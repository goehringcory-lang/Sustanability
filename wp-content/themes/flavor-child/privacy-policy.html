What this site collects

Very little. This is a small, simple site with no user accounts, no comments, no newsletter, and no ads. Here's exactly what gets collected:

Contact form. If you send me a message through the contact page, I receive your name, email address, and whatever you wrote. I use that information only to reply to you. I don't add you to any list or share your information with anyone.

Analytics. I use [Google Analytics / Plausible / Fathom] to understand how many people visit the site and which pages they read. This helps me know what's useful. [If using Plausible/Fathom: This tool doesn't use cookies and doesn't collect personal data. If using Google Analytics: Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish visitors. You can opt out by installing Google's browser add-on.]

Affiliate links. Some links on this site go to product pages on Amazon or brand websites. If you click one and buy something, I earn a small commission. The retailer may know you came from this site, but I don't receive any of your personal information from those transactions.

What this site does not do

  • Sell or share your personal data with anyone
  • Run display ads or retargeting
  • Use tracking cookies beyond essential analytics
  • Maintain user accounts or profiles
  • Send marketing emails

Your rights

Regardless of where you live, you can:

  • Ask what I have. If you've contacted me through the form, I can tell you exactly what information I have from you (it's just your message).
  • Ask me to delete it. I'll remove your contact form data promptly. Just email me.
  • Opt out of analytics. Use your browser's Do Not Track setting, an ad blocker, or the opt-out tools mentioned above.

If you're in the European Union (GDPR), you also have the right to data portability and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. If you're in California (CCPA), you have the right to know what personal information is collected, request its deletion, and not be discriminated against for exercising these rights. This site does not sell personal information.
